Optical Discs

Introduction

Welcome to our website about optical discs. Optical discs are a type of digital storage medium that have played a significant role in data storage and distribution.

Types of Optical Discs

Optical discs come in various formats, including CD (Compact Disc), DVD (Digital Versatile Disc), and Blu-ray. Each type has its own storage capacity and specific uses.

History

Optical discs have a rich history, dating back to the late 1970s. They have evolved over the years, from CDs for audio to DVDs for video and data storage, and finally to high-capacity Blu-ray discs.
